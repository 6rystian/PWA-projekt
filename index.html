kVr discography

This is the list of selected, most significant albums created and released by kVr. The full list with streaming and download is available here.

The Light (2021)

The last project under the moniker of kVr. It went through by far the longest production process, starting as early as 2015 and ultimately being finished in December of 2020. The second full-length album after 2018's "Nothing" (excluding the compilations).


Released: April 18, 2021

Producers: kVr

Length: 45:22, 11 tracks

Neverlast (2020)

A second compilation of kVr songs released as a physical CD. All but one track were released in the same year of 2020. The compilation contains full "Letdown" and "The Storm Beofre the Calm" EPs, as well as three songs produced by Mors (each one from a different part of the "Kill the Sun" trilogy) and 2 bonus tracks, one being from 2018.


Released: December 29, 2020

Producers: kVr, Mors

Length: 43:05, 15 tracks

Kill the Sun 3 (2020)

Third and last installment of experimental series "Kill the Sun". This EP drifts away from the typical alternative rap sounds, as it has been majorly influenced by indie pop/rock and most songs can be put somewhere around these subgenres.


Released: December 23, 2020

Producers: wurlishmouk, Bjorn Llaneza, Mors, Imotape Productions

Length: 13:33, 4 tracks

Kill the Sun (2020)

First part of the "Kill the Sun" trilogy. Put together mostly from throw-away bars and verses from ca. 2017 used on guitar-heavy instrumentals


Released: June 6, 2020

Producers: Systematik, CUTBOI, Mors

Length: 10:35, 4 tracks

Letdown (2020)

The first of 5 EPs released that year during the build-up to "The Light" album.


Released: April 4, 2020

Producers: kVr

Length: 18:52, 6 tracks

Undecim (2019)

First physical CD released by kVr on the first anniversary of "Nothing". Contains all self-produced tracks from "Nothing", full "Lost in Time" EP (+ a remix of the title song) and 5 bonus, unreleased songs.


Released: November 25, 2019

Producers: kVr

Length: 50:30, 18 tracks

Lost in Time (2019)

First fully self-produced project. The concept of the EP is that all of the melodies besides the outro were first created in the first months of 2016, to be specifically selected and re-produced 3 years later for the purpose of this project.


Released: June 11, 2019

Producers: kVr

Length: 13:27, 6 tracks

Nothing (2018)

First full-length album by kVr and his second ever after 2015's "Mic Check EP". Written in 2016 and recorded in 2017, this is still the most important project in the discography, showing the growth as an artist and as a person within the few years that had passed since the early days of kVr's adventure with music, as much as it was needed as an outlet for personal struggles of growing up as a turbulent teenager. The album has 14 concept-core tracks and 4 bonus tracks, three from each being the first self-produced songs released.


Released: November 25, 2018

Producers: kVr, Contrary, Yes Beatz, JurdBeats, NoCredit, BruceWayne, FiFtY VinC, SimpTunes, Beatfella, T.M.Vermehren

Length: 68:28, 18 tracks
